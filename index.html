- LING YUAN YUAN -

貫穿五欲六塵的層層煙迷，
俯首越過靜謐陰罹；
静聽內在神性靈喚，
照鑒明台真理。

靈 元靈本源 Spirituality: That we inherit from the Source
元 自性本始 Oneness: Who we are from the Beginning
院 謐静空間 Temple: Where we enjoy the Silence

空無、幽玄、静穆，
一廂大隱隱於市，人神相會之靈山秘境。
With spacious emptiness, delicate subtleness and silent respectfulness,
it is a spiritual mountain, a secret space where man and divinity meet,
quietly hidden in a busy city.
